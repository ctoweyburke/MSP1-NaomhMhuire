About

Set up in 2003, Naomh Mhuire has grown from year to year. In 2013, the Club took part in the Gaelic4Girls Programme and the success of these players was seen in 2019 when the players won Galway Junior B Club Final and a year later in 2020 they won the Galway Junior A Club Final.
